## Appointment Policy
- Appointments must be booked at least 24 hours in advance
- New patient appointments require a 45-minute initial exam
- Cancellation or rescheduling must be done at least 24 hours before the appointment
- Late cancellation fee: $50
- No-show fee: $75
- Walk-ins accepted for emergencies only

## Insurance & Payment
- We accept all major dental insurance plans
- Direct billing available to most insurance providers
- Payment plans available for treatments over $500 (up to 12 months interest-free)
- Accepted payment methods: Cash, Visa, Mastercard, Debit, e-Transfer

## Patient Guidelines
- Please arrive 10 minutes early for your appointment
- Bring your insurance card and government ID to your first visit
- Inform us of any medications you are taking
- Tell us about any dental anxiety — we offer sedation options
- Children under 16 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian
