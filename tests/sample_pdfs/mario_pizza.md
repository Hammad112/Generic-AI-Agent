# Mario's Italiano Pizzeria

## About Us
Mario's Italiano Pizzeria is a family-owned Italian restaurant located at 234 Main Street, Toronto, ON M5V 2K4. We have been serving authentic Italian food since 1995. Our restaurant seats 45 customers and also offers takeout and delivery service within a 10km radius.

Phone: (416) 555-0142
Email: orders@mariospizzeria.ca
Website: www.mariospizzeria.ca

## Business Hours
- Monday to Thursday: 11:00 AM – 10:00 PM
- Friday and Saturday: 11:00 AM – 11:30 PM
- Sunday: 12:00 PM – 9:00 PM
- Holidays: Closed on Christmas Day and New Year's Day. Reduced hours on other holidays (12–6 PM).

## Delivery Policy
- Free delivery on orders over $30 within 5km
- $3.99 delivery fee for orders under $30
- $5.99 delivery fee for 5–10km radius
- No delivery beyond 10km
- Estimated delivery time: 30–45 minutes
- Address is required and must include a valid postal code

## Pickup
- Orders can be picked up at 234 Main Street
- Usual wait time: 15–25 minutes after placing the order
- Please have your order number ready

## Loyalty Program
- Earn 10 points for every dollar spent
- 500 points = $5 discount
- 1000 points = $10 discount + free garlic bread
- Gold members (1500+ points): 10% off all orders

## Allergen Information
- All our pizzas contain gluten and dairy
- We use nuts in some desserts (Tiramisu)
- Gluten-free pizza base available for +$3.00
- Please inform us of any allergies before ordering

## Cancellation Policy
- Orders can be cancelled within 5 minutes of placing
- After preparation begins, no cancellation is possible
- Refunds processed within 3–5 business days
