## 5. Test Drives & Appointments
- Test drives must be booked in advance.
- Customers must be at least 21 years old and possess a valid driver's license.
- Test drives are limited to 30 minutes.
- To book, we require the customer's full name, preferred vehicle, and requested date/time.

## 6. Financing & Trade-Ins
- We work with multiple lenders to secure the best rates.
- Pre-approval applications can be started online or in-store.
- Trade-in appraisals take approximately 20 minutes and require the vehicle title.

## 7. Delivery & Purchase Rules
- Vehicles can be delivered to the customer's home within a 50-mile radius for a flat fee of $100.
- Out-of-state deliveries are handled via third-party transport (quotes provided upon request).
- A non-refundable deposit of $500 is required to hold a vehicle for up to 3 days.

## 8. Customer Support & Complaints
- All vehicles come with a standard 3-year/36,000-mile warranty.
- For issues within 7 days of purchase, please contact our post-sales support team immediately.
